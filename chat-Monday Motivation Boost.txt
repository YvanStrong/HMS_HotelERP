1.  Executive Summary

This document defines the comprehensive requirements and feature specifications for a Hotel Management System (HMS). The system is designed to digitally unify and automate all core hotel operations — from guest check-in to pool reservations, inventory tracking, billing, and compliance — within a single, scalable platform.

The HMS will serve front-desk staff, housekeeping teams, food & beverage managers, finance controllers, and senior management, enabling real-time decision-making and exceptional guest experiences.

Scope:  This HMS covers 9 functional modules serving all departments of a full-service hotel property. The system is designed to scale from boutique hotels to large resort chains.

2.  Room Management
The Room Management module is the operational heart of the HMS. It manages the entire lifecycle of a hotel room — from online booking and assignment through check-in, stay, and check-out — with full real-time visibility.

Core Features
‣	Online Booking System: Enable guests to book rooms from the hotel website, mobile app, or OTA channels. Specify dates, room type (single, double, suite, deluxe), number of guests, and special preferences.
◦	Support for group bookings and corporate blocks
◦	Integration with Google Hotel Ads and OTAs (Booking.com, Expedia)
‣	Real-time Room Availability: Live calendar view of all rooms with current status. Prevents overbooking through a centralized availability engine synchronized across all booking channels.
‣	Check-in / Check-out Management: Efficient digital processing of arrivals and departures, including early check-ins, late check-outs, and express checkout via mobile.
◦	Digital ID verification and e-signature support
◦	Automated pre-arrival communication with digital key delivery
‣	Automated Room Assignment: Intelligently assign rooms based on guest tier, preferences, floor preferences, proximity requests, and real-time availability.
‣	Dynamic Rate Management: Yield management engine adjusting pricing based on occupancy, seasonality, competitor rates, and special events or promotions.
◦	Revenue management dashboard with pricing recommendations
◦	Integration with channel managers for consistent rate parity
‣	Housekeeping & Room Status: Track every room across statuses: Occupied, Vacant-Clean, Vacant-Dirty, Inspected, Out of Order, Under Maintenance.
◦	Mobile app for housekeeping staff to update status in real-time
◦	Supervisor inspection workflow with photo evidence capability
‣	Do Not Disturb (DND) Integration: Guests can set DND via room controls, mobile app, or front desk. Staff are automatically notified and rooms are skipped in housekeeping routes.
‣	Room Blocking & Holds: Place courtesy holds, maintenance blocks, or VIP pre-assignments with expiry timers and automatic release rules.

3.  Pool & Recreational Facilities Management
This module manages all recreational amenities including swimming pools, spa, gym, and other leisure facilities. It ensures optimal utilization, safety compliance, and a premium guest experience.

Core Features
‣	Online Booking & Slot Reservations: Guests can reserve time slots for the pool, spa, or gym via the mobile app or front desk. Configurable slot durations and buffer times between sessions.
◦	QR code access passes generated on booking confirmation
◦	Waitlist management when slots are full
‣	Access Control Integration: Digital check-in at facility entry. Logs guest identity, entry/exit times, and duration. Integrated with wristband or key card systems.
‣	Capacity Management: Set and enforce maximum occupancy per facility. Real-time counters with automated alerts to staff when nearing capacity limits.
◦	Display boards at facility entrances showing live capacity
‣	Maintenance & Hygiene Scheduling: Automated maintenance windows with blackout periods preventing bookings. Water quality logs, chemical balance records, and inspector sign-off workflows.
◦	Preventive maintenance task assignment and tracking
◦	Compliance reporting for health inspections
‣	Cabana & Sunbed Reservations: Assign and manage premium poolside seating, cabanas, and loungers with optional add-on services (towels, beverages, sunscreen).
‣	Lifeguard & Staff Scheduling: Manage lifeguard rosters, certification expiry tracking, and incident reporting integrated with the facility management module.
‣	Revenue Tracking: Track revenue from paid pool access, spa treatments, cabana rentals, and F&B orders poolside.

 
4.  Inventory Management
A centralized inventory system tracking all hotel supplies across departments — housekeeping, F&B, maintenance, and front office. Ensures optimal stock levels, prevents pilferage, and enables cost-efficient procurement.

Core Features
‣	Multi-department Stock Tracking: Maintain separate inventory registers for housekeeping (linens, toiletries), F&B (food, beverages, dry goods), maintenance (tools, spare parts), and front office (stationery).
‣	Automated Reorder Alerts: Configurable minimum stock thresholds per item. Automatic purchase order generation or alert to procurement team when levels fall below threshold.
◦	Email/SMS notifications to department heads and procurement
◦	Priority flagging for critical or high-usage items
‣	Consumption Rate Analytics: Track usage patterns by department, season, and room occupancy to forecast demand and optimize order quantities.
‣	Supplier & Purchase Order Management: Maintain a supplier directory with contracts, lead times, and pricing. Raise, approve, and track purchase orders through a digital workflow.
◦	Multi-level approval workflows for purchases above threshold amounts
◦	Supplier performance scoring based on delivery and quality
‣	Barcode & QR Code Scanning: Staff can log stock receipts, transfers, and consumption using barcode scanners or mobile app cameras — eliminating manual entry errors.
‣	Waste & Spoilage Tracking: Record and categorize waste events to identify loss patterns and implement corrective actions.
‣	Asset Register Integration: Track fixed assets (furniture, appliances, linen sets) alongside consumables with depreciation schedules and replacement forecasting.

 
5.  Invoice & Billing System
A comprehensive billing engine that automates guest invoicing from arrival to departure, integrates with multiple payment channels, and ensures tax compliance for both domestic and international guests.

Core Features
‣	Automatic Invoice Generation: Consolidated bill created at checkout reflecting room charges, F&B, spa, minibar, parking, telephone, and any incidental charges.
‣	Split Billing: Divide bills between multiple guests, company accounts, or payment methods. Handle partial payments and deposit allocations.
◦	Room-sharing billing splits with individual or shared charges
‣	Payment Gateway Integration: Seamless integration with major payment processors for credit/debit cards, mobile money, bank transfers, and digital wallets.
◦	PCI-DSS compliant card storage for repeat guests
◦	Contactless and QR code payment support
‣	Tax & Levy Management: Automatic calculation of applicable taxes (VAT, tourism levies, service charges) based on item type and jurisdiction. Tax-exempt handling for corporate or diplomatic guests.
‣	Multi-currency & FX Support: Accept payment in any currency with real-time exchange rate integration. Automatically convert and display amounts in hotel base currency.
‣	Discount & Promotion Engine: Apply negotiated corporate rates, loyalty discounts, promotional codes, and package inclusions. Full audit trail of all discounts applied.
‣	Proforma & Final Invoices: Generate proforma invoices for advance confirmation or credit facilities, and final tax invoices with hotel's fiscal details for corporate reimbursement.
‣	Night Audit Automation: End-of-day reconciliation posting room charges to all occupied rooms, generating balance reports and flagging discrepancies automatically.

 
6.  Guest Management & CRM
A powerful guest relationship management module that builds rich guest profiles, enables personalized service delivery, and drives loyalty and repeat bookings.

Core Features
‣	Unified Guest Profiles: Centralized profiles containing contact details, ID records, stay history, preferences (pillow type, floor, dietary, language), special occasions, and feedback history.
◦	Profile merge for duplicate records
◦	GDPR-compliant consent management and data retention policies
‣	Loyalty & Rewards Program: Points-based loyalty engine awarding guests for stays, F&B spend, and spa services. Tiered membership (Silver, Gold, Platinum) with escalating benefits.
◦	Points redemption for room upgrades, discounts, or complimentary services
◦	Partner program integration for airline miles and travel rewards
‣	Pre-arrival Personalization: Automated pre-arrival email/SMS with check-in instructions, upsell offers (room upgrades, airport transfers), and preference confirmations.
‣	In-stay Service Requests: Guests can make requests (extra pillows, room service, wake-up calls, laundry) via mobile app or in-room tablet, routed directly to the relevant department.
‣	Post-stay Engagement: Automated thank-you emails with feedback survey links. Re-engagement campaigns for past guests with personalized return offers.
‣	VIP & Blacklist Management: Flag VIP guests for priority treatment workflows. Maintain a sensitive incidents register for security awareness.
‣	Group & Event Management: Handle group bookings with rooming lists, event schedules, banquet requirements, and group billing under a single master folio.

 
7.  Food & Beverage Management
An integrated F&B module covering restaurant reservations, room service orders, banqueting, and bar operations — all linked to inventory and billing for seamless operations.

Core Features
‣	Restaurant Reservation System: Manage table bookings for in-house restaurants with floor plan visualization, dietary preference recording, and wait-list management.
‣	Room Service Order Management: Guests order via mobile app, in-room tablet, or telephone. Orders routed to kitchen display system (KDS) with real-time status tracking.
◦	Estimated delivery time display and guest notification on delivery
‣	Point of Sale (POS) Integration: Seamless POS terminals at restaurants, bars, and room service. Charges automatically posted to the guest's room folio.
‣	Menu Engineering & Costing: Digital menu management with recipe costing, margin analysis, and seasonal menu updates. Integration with inventory to track ingredient consumption.
‣	Banquet & Events Management: Plan and manage banquet events with event-specific menus, seating arrangements, AV requirements, and event-day staffing schedules.
‣	Dietary & Allergen Management: Flag dishes with allergen information. Guests can record dietary restrictions in their profile for kitchen awareness on all food orders.

 
8.  Reporting & Analytics
A data intelligence layer providing management with actionable insights across all hotel operations. Pre-built dashboards and ad hoc reporting capabilities support both operational and strategic decisions.

Core Reports
‣	Occupancy & RevPAR Reports: Daily, weekly, monthly occupancy rates, Average Daily Rate (ADR), Revenue Per Available Room (RevPAR), and pickup pace vs prior periods.
‣	Financial Reports: Income statements, revenue by department, outstanding accounts, collection efficiency, and month-end reconciliation reports.
‣	Housekeeping Performance: Room turnaround times, cleaning efficiency by housekeeper, DND impact analysis, and maintenance resolution times.
‣	Guest Analytics: New vs returning guest ratios, nationality mix, booking channel analysis, average length of stay, and repeat guest lifetime value.
‣	Inventory & Procurement Reports: Consumption trends, stock variance analysis, wastage reports, supplier performance, and procurement cost tracking.
‣	Guest Feedback & NPS: Aggregated survey scores, Net Promoter Score (NPS) trends, review monitoring from OTAs and Google, and complaint resolution tracking.
‣	Forecast & Budget Variance: Actual performance vs budget and forecast, with variance analysis and trend-based demand forecasting for the next 30/60/90 days.

Executive Dashboard:  A real-time command center view accessible on desktop, tablet, and mobile showing live occupancy, revenue, issues, and KPIs at a glance.

 
9.  Additional System Features

Staff & HR Integration
‣	Role-Based Access Control (RBAC): Granular permissions by job role. Receptionists, housekeepers, F&B staff, and managers only see the modules relevant to their function.
‣	Shift Scheduling: Digital shift planner integrated with the HMS allowing department heads to schedule, swap, and track staff attendance.
‣	Staff Performance Metrics: Track individual KPIs for front desk (check-in speed, upsell success), housekeeping (rooms per shift, quality scores), and F&B (order accuracy, delivery times).

Mobile & Digital Guest Experience
‣	Guest Mobile App: Full-featured app for booking, mobile check-in/out, digital key access, service requests, F&B ordering, and account review.
‣	In-room Digital Concierge: Tablet-based system providing room controls, hotel information, local attractions, and direct service ordering — reducing front desk call volume.
‣	Digital Signage Integration: Manage content on lobby screens, restaurant menu boards, and elevator displays from within the HMS.

Integrations & Connectivity
‣	Channel Manager: Synchronize availability and rates across OTAs, GDS channels, and the hotel's direct booking engine in real time.
‣	Accounting Software Integration: Export financial data to accounting platforms (e.g., QuickBooks, Sage, SAP) for streamlined finance operations.
‣	IoT & Smart Room Integration: Connect to smart room controls (temperature, lighting, curtains) and energy management systems to enable automation and sustainability tracking.
‣	Offline Mode & Data Sync: The system remains operational during internet outages. All transactions are queued and synchronized automatically once connectivity is restored.
‣	API & Webhook Support: Open REST API and webhook infrastructure enabling custom integrations with third-party systems, loyalty platforms, and property add-ons.

 
10.  Security & Data Privacy
Security is built into every layer of the HMS — from data encryption and access controls to compliance with international data protection laws. All guest data is treated with the highest standard of confidentiality.

Security Architecture
‣	End-to-End Data Encryption: AES-256 encryption for all data at rest; TLS 1.3 for all data in transit. Payment card data tokenized in compliance with PCI-DSS standards.
‣	Role-Based Access Control: Principle of least privilege enforced across all user accounts. Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA) required for admin and manager accounts.
‣	Audit Trail & Activity Logs: Immutable logs of all user actions, data accesses, configuration changes, and financial transactions. Available for compliance review and forensic investigation.
‣	Automated Backups & Disaster Recovery: Automated daily backups with geo-redundant storage. Recovery Point Objective (RPO) of 1 hour and Recovery Time Objective (RTO) of 4 hours.
‣	Intrusion Detection & Monitoring: 24/7 system monitoring with anomaly detection, failed login alerts, and automated account lockout after repeated failed authentication attempts.

Data Privacy & Compliance
‣	GDPR Compliance: Guest consent management, right-to-erasure workflows, data portability exports, and Data Processing Agreement templates for third-party vendors.
‣	PCI-DSS Compliance: Quarterly vulnerability scans, annual penetration testing, and SAQ-D compliance for cardholder data environment.
‣	Local Regulatory Compliance: Configurable compliance settings for local hospitality data laws, government guest registration requirements (e.g., passport/ID logging for immigration compliance).

Security Certifications:  The HMS is designed to achieve ISO 27001 certification and comply with PCI-DSS Level 1 requirements. Security assessment reports are available upon request.

## 🎯 ARCHITECTURE OVERVIEW

### Multi-Tenant SaaS Model
```
🔹 SUPER ADMIN (YOU - Platform Owner)
   ├── Manages: All hotels (tenants), subscriptions, platform settings
   ├── DOES NOT manage: Individual rooms, reservations, hotel operations
   └── Revenue: Hotels pay subscription → you provide isolated tenant instance

🔹 HOTEL TENANT (Your Customer)
   ├── Hotel Admin: Full access to THEIR hotel's data only
   ├── Staff Roles: Receptionist, Housekeeping, F&B, Finance, Manager
   └── Data Isolation: hotel_id scoped in EVERY query (Hibernate @Where clause)

🔹 GUEST/BOOKER (End User)
   ├── Books rooms, manages reservations, checks in/out
   └── Limited API access via public endpoints + JWT guest token
```

### Tech Stack Alignment
| Layer | Technology | Purpose |
|-------|-----------|---------|
| Backend | Spring Boot 3.x + Hibernate 6 | REST API, Business Logic, JPA |
| Database | PostgreSQL 15 + Redis | Relational data + caching/session |
| Auth | Spring Security + JWT + OAuth2 | RBAC, multi-tenant auth |
| Frontend | Next.js 14 (App Router) + TypeScript | Admin dashboards, guest portal |
| Real-time | WebSocket/STOMP + Redis Pub/Sub | Live room status, notifications |
| File Storage | AWS S3 / MinIO | Room photos, ID documents, invoices |

## 🚪 ROOM MANAGEMENT MODULE - COMPLETE SPECIFICATION

### 📦 Core Entities (JPA Models)

```java
// 1. Hotel (Tenant Root)
@Entity
@Table(name = "hotels")
public class Hotel {
    @Id @GeneratedValue UUID id;
    String name, code, timezone, currency;
    @Enumerated(STRING) SubscriptionStatus status; // ACTIVE, TRIAL, SUSPENDED
    @OneToMany(mappedBy = "hotel") List<Room> rooms;
    // ... audit fields, settings
}

// 2. Room Type (Template)
@Entity
@Table(name = "room_types")
public class RoomType {
    @Id @GeneratedValue UUID id;
    @ManyToOne Hotel hotel; // Tenant isolation
    String code, name, description;
    Integer maxOccupancy, bedCount;
    BigDecimal baseRate;
    @ElementCollection List<String> amenities; // ["wifi", "ac", "minibar"]
    @OneToMany List<Room> rooms;
}

// 3. Room (Physical Instance)
@Entity
@Table(name = "rooms")
@Where(clause = "deleted = false") // Soft delete
public class Room {
    @Id @GeneratedValue UUID id;
    @ManyToOne Hotel hotel;
    @ManyToOne RoomType roomType;
    String roomNumber, floor, building;
    
    @Enumerated(STRING) RoomStatus status; // See state machine below
    @Enumerated(STRING) CleanlinessStatus cleanliness; // DIRTY, CLEAN, INSPECTED
    
    Boolean isOutOfOrder;
    String maintenanceNotes;
    
    @OneToMany(mappedBy = "room") List<Reservation> reservations;
    @OneToMany(mappedBy = "room") List<RoomCharge> charges; // minibar, extras
}

// 4. Room Status Enum (State Machine)
public enum RoomStatus {
    AVAILABLE,      // Ready to book
    RESERVED,       // Booked but guest not arrived
    CHECKED_IN,     // Guest occupying
    CHECKED_OUT,    // Guest left, pending cleaning
    OUT_OF_ORDER,   // Maintenance/repair
    BLOCKED         // Admin hold (staff use, VIP prep)
}

// 5. Reservation (Booking Lifecycle)
@Entity
@Table(name = "reservations")
public class Reservation {
    @Id @GeneratedValue UUID id;
    @ManyToOne Hotel hotel;
    @ManyToOne Room room;
    @ManyToOne Guest guest;
    
    LocalDate checkInDate, checkOutDate;
    LocalDateTime actualCheckIn, actualCheckOut;
    
    Integer adults, children;
    BigDecimal nightlyRate, totalAmount;
    
    @Enumerated(STRING) ReservationStatus status; // CONFIRMED, CHECKED_IN, CHECKED_OUT, CANCELLED
    String specialRequests, source; // "direct", "booking.com", "corporate"
    
    @OneToMany(mappedBy = "reservation") List<RoomCharge> charges;
    @OneToOne Invoice invoice;
}

// 6. RoomCharge (Consumptions during stay)
@Entity
public class RoomCharge {
    @Id @GeneratedValue UUID id;
    @ManyToOne Reservation reservation;
    @ManyToOne Room room;
    
    String description; // "Minibar: Coca-Cola x2", "Laundry: Shirt"
    BigDecimal amount;
    LocalDateTime timestamp;
    
    @Enumerated(STRING) ChargeType type; // MINIBAR, FNB, LAUNDRY, PHONE, OTHER
    String postedBy; // staff user ID who logged it
}
```

### 🔄 Room Lifecycle State Machine
```
[AVAILABLE] 
    │
    ├──(Guest Books)──▶ [RESERVED] 
    │       │
    │       ├──(Guest Arrives)──▶ [CHECKED_IN] 
    │       │       │
    │       │       ├──(Charge Added)──▶ [CHECKED_IN]* (status unchanged, charges accumulate)
    │       │       │
    │       │       └──(Guest Checks Out)──▶ [CHECKED_OUT]
    │       │               │
    │       │               └──(Housekeeping Cleans)──▶ [AVAILABLE]
    │       │
    │       └──(Cancelled)──▶ [AVAILABLE]
    │
    ├──(Admin Block)──▶ [BLOCKED] ──(Release)──▶ [AVAILABLE]
    │
    └──(Maintenance)──▶ [OUT_OF_ORDER] ──(Repair Done)──▶ [AVAILABLE]
```

## 🔗 INTEGRATION POINTS: ROOM + OTHER MODULES

The Room module doesn't exist in isolation. Here's how it connects:

| Module | Integration Point | Data Flow | API/Webhook |
|--------|------------------|-----------|------------|
| **Guest Management** | Reservation creation requires guest profile | Guest ID ↔ Reservation | `POST /guests` → returns guest_id for reservation |
| **Billing/Invoice** | Room charges + reservation totals feed invoice engine | RoomCharge → Invoice line items | Webhook: `room.charge.posted` → billing service |
| **F&B Module** | Minibar/room service orders post charges to room | F&B Order → RoomCharge | `POST /rooms/{id}/charges` with type=FNB |
| **Housekeeping** | Check-out triggers cleaning task; status updates affect availability | Room.status → Housekeeping queue | WebSocket: `room.status.changed` |
| **Inventory** | Minibar consumption triggers stock deduction | RoomCharge (MINIBAR) → Inventory decrement | Async event: `minibar.consumed` |
| **Reporting** | Room occupancy, ADR, RevPAR calculated from reservations | Reservation + Room data → Analytics DB | Scheduled job: nightly aggregation |
| **Mobile App** | Guests view room status, request services, digital key | Room data → Guest app via filtered API | Guest-scoped endpoints: `GET /guest/reservations` |

### Critical Cross-Module Events (Use Domain Events / Kafka)
```java
// Example: When room status changes to CHECKED_OUT
@EventListener
public void onRoomCheckedOut(RoomStatusChangedEvent event) {
    // 1. Create housekeeping task
    housekeepingService.createCleaningTask(event.getRoomId());
    
    // 2. Notify billing: finalize provisional invoice
    billingService.provisionalInvoice(event.getReservationId());
    
    // 3. Update inventory: flag minibar for restock check
    if (event.getRoom().hasMinibar()) {
        inventoryService.flagForRestock(event.getRoomId(), "MINIBAR");
    }
    
    // 4. Broadcast to frontend via WebSocket
    websocketTemplate.convertAndSend(
        "/topic/hotel/" + event.getHotelId() + "/rooms",
        new RoomUpdateDTO(event.getRoomId(), event.getNewStatus())
    );
}
```

### 📦 Core Entities (JPA Models)

```java
// Facility (Pool, Spa, Gym, etc.)
@Entity
@Table(name = "facilities")
public class Facility {
    @Id @GeneratedValue UUID id;
    @ManyToOne Hotel hotel; // Tenant isolation
    
    String name, code, description;
    @Enumerated(STRING) FacilityType type; // POOL, SPA, GYM, SAUNA, TENNIS
    
    Integer maxCapacity; // max guests at once
    Integer slotDurationMinutes; // default 60
    Integer bufferMinutesBetweenSlots; // cleaning time
    
    BigDecimal basePrice; // 0 = free for guests
    Boolean requiresAdvanceBooking;
    Boolean allowsWalkIn;
    
    @ElementCollection List<String> amenities; // ["towels", "lockers", "showers"]
    @OneToMany(mappedBy = "facility") List<FacilitySlot> slots;
    
    // Operating hours (JSONB or separate table for complex schedules)
    @ElementCollection Map<DayOfWeek, LocalTime> openTime;
    @ElementCollection Map<DayOfWeek, LocalTime> closeTime;
}

// FacilitySlot (Bookable time window)
@Entity
@Table(name = "facility_slots", uniqueConstraints = @UniqueConstraint(columnNames = {"facility_id", "start_time"}))
public class FacilitySlot {
    @Id @GeneratedValue UUID id;
    @ManyToOne Facility facility;
    
    LocalDateTime startTime, endTime;
    Integer currentBookings, maxBookings; // for capacity management
    
    @Enumerated(STRING) SlotStatus status; // AVAILABLE, BOOKED, MAINTENANCE, CLOSED
    
    @OneToMany(mappedBy = "slot") List<FacilityBooking> bookings;
    
    // Computed field (transient)
    @Transient
    public boolean isAvailable() {
        return status == SlotStatus.AVAILABLE && currentBookings < maxBookings;
    }
}

// FacilityBooking (Guest reservation)
@Entity
@Table(name = "facility_bookings")
public class FacilityBooking {
    @Id @GeneratedValue UUID id;
    @ManyToOne FacilitySlot slot;
    @ManyToOne Reservation reservation; // null for walk-in guests
    @ManyToOne Guest guest; // required if no reservation
    
    String bookingReference; // "POOL-2026-XK9"
    LocalDateTime bookedAt;
    
    @Enumerated(STRING) BookingStatus status; // CONFIRMED, CHECKED_IN, COMPLETED, CANCELLED, NO_SHOW
    
    // Access control
    String qrCode, accessCode; // for entry validation
    LocalDateTime checkedInAt, checkedOutAt;
    
    // Charges
    BigDecimal amountPaid;
    @Enumerated(STRING) PaymentStatus paymentStatus; // PENDING, PAID, REFUNDED
    
    // Link to room charges if applicable
    @ManyToOne RoomCharge roomCharge; // if charged to room folio
}

// Facility Maintenance Log
@Entity
public class FacilityMaintenance {
    @Id @GeneratedValue UUID id;
    @ManyToOne Facility facility;
    
    String title, description, reportedBy;
    @Enumerated(STRING) PriorityLevel priority; // LOW, MEDIUM, HIGH, CRITICAL
    
    LocalDateTime reportedAt, scheduledStart, scheduledEnd, completedAt;
    @Enumerated(STRING) MaintenanceStatus status; // REPORTED, SCHEDULED, IN_PROGRESS, COMPLETED
    
    @ElementCollection List<String> affectedSlots; // slot IDs blocked during maintenance
    BigDecimal cost; // for reporting
}
```

// When posting a facility charge to room folio
@PostMapping("/facilities/bookings/{bookingId}/charge-to-room")
@PreAuthorize("hasRole('RECEPTIONIST') or hasRole('FINANCE')")
public ResponseEntity<RoomChargeDTO> chargeFacilityToRoom(
    @PathVariable UUID bookingId,
    @PathVariable UUID hotelId,
    @RequestBody ChargeToRoomRequest request) {
    
    // 1. Validate booking belongs to active reservation
    FacilityBooking booking = facilityService.getBooking(bookingId, hotelId);
    if (!booking.getReservation().getStatus().equals(ReservationStatus.CHECKED_IN)) {
        throw new BusinessException("Only active reservations can be charged");
    }
    
    // 2. Create room charge via Room module API (internal service call)
    RoomCharge charge = roomChargeService.create(
        booking.getReservation().getId(),
        booking.getFacility().getName() + " Access",
        booking.getAmountPaid(),
        ChargeType.RECREATION,
        request.getPostedBy()
    );
    
    // 3. Link booking to charge
    booking.setRoomCharge(charge);
    facilityBookingRepository.save(booking);
    
    // 4. Broadcast to guest folio (WebSocket)
    websocketTemplate.convertAndSend(
        "/topic/reservations/" + booking.getReservation().getId() + "/folio",
        new FolioUpdateEvent(charge)
    );
    
    return ResponseEntity.ok(RoomChargeDTO.fromEntity(charge));
}

### 🔄 Integration with Room Module (Minibar Auto-Reorder)
```java
// Event listener: When room is checked out, trigger minibar restock check
@EventListener
public void onRoomCheckedOut(RoomStatusChangedEvent event) {
    if (!event.getRoom().hasMinibar()) return;
    
    // 1. Get all minibar items consumed during this stay
    List<RoomCharge> minibarCharges = roomChargeService.findByReservationAndType(
        event.getReservationId(), 
        ChargeType.MINIBAR
    );
    
    // 2. For each consumed item, decrement room-level minibar stock
    for (RoomCharge charge : minibarCharges) {
        InventoryItem item = inventoryService.getBySku(charge.getProductSku());
        
        // Room-level minibar inventory (separate from main store)
        RoomMinibarStock roomStock = roomMinibarRepo.findByRoomAndItem(
            event.getRoomId(), item.getId()
        );
        roomStock.decrementStock(charge.getQuantity());
        roomMinibarRepo.save(roomStock);
        
        // 3. If room stock below threshold, flag for restock
        if (roomStock.getCurrentStock() <= item.getMinibarReorderThreshold()) {
            housekeepingService.createRestockTask(
                event.getRoomId(), 
                item.getId(), 
                item.getMinimumStock() - roomStock.getCurrentStock()
            );
        }
    }
}
```

// During room assignment in ReservationService
public Room assignRoomToReservation(Reservation reservation, AssignmentCriteria criteria) {
    // 1. Get guest preferences
    GuestPreferences prefs = guestService.getPreferences(reservation.getGuestId());
    
    // 2. Build room query with preference weights
    RoomQuery query = RoomQuery.builder()
        .hotelId(reservation.getHotelId())
        .roomTypeId(reservation.getRoomTypeId())
        .dates(reservation.getCheckInDate(), reservation.getCheckOutDate())
        .preferences(prefs) // adds weighted filters
        .build();
    
    // 3. Find best match
    Room assignedRoom = roomRepository.findBestMatch(query);
    
    // 4. If room found, prepare preference-based amenities
    if (assignedRoom != null && criteria.applyPreferences()) {
        preferenceService.prepareRoomForGuest(assignedRoom.getId(), prefs);
        
        // 5. Flag any critical alerts (allergies, accessibility)
        if (prefs.hasCriticalAlerts()) {
            alertService.createRoomAlert(
                assignedRoom.getId(),
                AlertType.GUEST_PREFERENCE,
                prefs.getCriticalAlerts(),
                reservation.getId()
            );
        }
    }
    
    return assignedRoom;
}

// When F&B order is charged to room
@Service
public class FbRoomChargeIntegration {
    
    @Autowired private RoomChargeService roomChargeService;
    @Autowired private WebSocketMessagingTemplate websocketTemplate;
    
    @Transactional
    public RoomCharge postOrderToRoomFolio(Order order, UUID reservationId) {
        // 1. Validate reservation is active
        Reservation reservation = reservationService.getActiveReservation(reservationId);
        if (reservation == null) {
            throw new BusinessException("Cannot charge to inactive reservation");
        }
        
        // 2. Create room charge
        RoomCharge charge = roomChargeService.create(
            reservationId,
            order.getOutlet().getName() + " - " + order.getOrderNumber(),
            order.getTotal(),
            ChargeType.FNB,
            order.getCreatedBy()
        );
        
        // 3. Link order to charge
        order.setRoomCharge(charge);
        order.setPaymentStatus(PaymentStatus.CHARGED_TO_ROOM);
        orderRepository.save(order);
        
        // 4. Real-time folio update via WebSocket
        FolioUpdateEvent event = FolioUpdateEvent.builder()
            .reservationId(reservationId)
            .charge(RoomChargeDTO.fromEntity(charge))
            .newBalance(reservation.calculateBalance())
            .timestamp(LocalDateTime.now())
            .build();
            
        websocketTemplate.convertAndSend(
            "/topic/reservations/" + reservationId + "/folio",
            event
        );
        
        // 5. Log for audit
        auditService.logRoomCharge(
            reservation.getHotelId(),
            "F&B_ORDER_CHARGED",
            Map.of("orderId", order.getId(), "chargeId", charge.getId())
        );
        
        return charge;
    }
}

// Scheduled job: Nightly aggregation for reports
@Component
public class ReportAggregationJob {
    
    @Autowired private RoomRepository roomRepo;
    @Autowired private ReservationRepository reservationRepo;
    @Autowired private AnalyticsRepository analyticsRepo;
    
    @Scheduled(cron = "0 0 2 * * ?") // 2 AM daily
    @Transactional
    public void aggregateDailyReports() {
        LocalDate yesterday = LocalDate.now().minusDays(1);
        
        List<Hotel> hotels = hotelService.getAllActive();
        
        for (Hotel hotel : hotels) {
            // 1. Calculate occupancy metrics
            long totalRooms = roomRepo.countByHotelId(hotel.getId());
            long occupiedRooms = reservationRepo.countOccupiedRooms(hotel.getId(), yesterday);
            double occupancyRate = (double) occupiedRooms / totalRooms * 100;
            
            // 2. Calculate revenue metrics
            BigDecimal roomRevenue = reservationRepo.sumRevenueByDate(hotel.getId(), yesterday);
            BigDecimal revpar = roomRevenue.divide(new BigDecimal(totalRooms), 2, RoundingMode.HALF_UP);
            
            // 3. Save to analytics store
            OccupancyReport report = OccupancyReport.builder()
                .hotelId(hotel.getId())
                .date(yesterday)
                .totalRooms((int) totalRooms)
                .occupiedRooms((int) occupiedRooms)
                .occupancyRate(occupancyRate)
                .adr(roomRevenue.divide(new BigDecimal(occupiedRooms), 2, RoundingMode.HALF_UP))
                .revpar(revpar)
                .lastUpdated(LocalDateTime.now())
                .build();
                
            analyticsRepo.save(report);
            
            // 4. Trigger cache invalidation for real-time dashboards
            redisTemplate.delete("dashboard:" + hotel.getId() + ":live");
        }
    }
}

### 🔐 Super Admin Security Model
```java
// All platform endpoints require SUPER_ADMIN role + additional validation
@RestController
@RequestMapping("/api/v1/platform")
@PreAuthorize("hasRole('SUPER_ADMIN')")
public class PlatformAdminController {
    
    // Impersonation requires explicit reason logging
    @PostMapping("/tenants/{tenantId}/impersonate")
    @AuditLog(action = "IMPERSONATE_TENANT", target = "#tenantId")
    public ResponseEntity<ImpersonationResponse> impersonateTenant(
            @PathVariable UUID tenantId,
            @RequestBody @Valid ImpersonateRequest request,
            Authentication auth) {
        
        // 1. Validate tenant exists and is active
        PlatformTenant tenant = tenantService.getOrThrow(tenantId);
        if (tenant.getProvisioningStatus() != ProvisioningStatus.PROVISIONED) {
            throw new BusinessException("Cannot impersonate non-active tenant");
        }
        
        // 2. Create limited-scope token for hotel admin actions
        String impersonationToken = jwtService.createImpersonationToken(
            tenant.getHotel().getId(),
            request.getDuration(),
            request.getRestrictActions()
        );
        
        // 3. Log to platform audit trail
        platformAuditService.log(
            auth.getName(),
            "IMPERSONATE_TENANT",
            tenantId,
            Map.of("reason", request.getReason(), "duration", request.getDuration())
        );
        
        // 4. Notify tenant if requested
        if (request.isNotifyTenant()) {
            notificationService.sendImpersonationNotice(
                tenant.getHotel().getContactEmail(),
                request.getReason(),
                LocalDateTime.now().plusMinutes(request.getDuration())
            );
        }
        
        return ResponseEntity.ok(new ImpersonationResponse(impersonationToken, ...));
    }
}
```
